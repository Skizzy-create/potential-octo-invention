# potential-octo-invention

> Nothing what the name says — just the simple classic **ASTEROIDS GAME**. 🚀

> *Because sometimes the only cure for fun... is more fun.*

A simple, classic **Asteroids** game built purely for fun. Pilot your ship through a field of tumbling space rocks, blast them to bits, and see how long you can survive the cosmos — no plot, no stress, just pure arcade bliss.

## 🎮 About

This is a no-frills recreation of the timeless arcade classic **Asteroids**. Made from scratch for the love of the game, it's the kind of project that starts with "I wonder if I can..." and ends with you losing three hours trying to beat your own high score.

## ✨ Features

- Classic asteroid-blasting gameplay
- Smooth ship controls
- Progressively challenging waves
- Satisfying *pew pew* action

## 🕹️ How to Play

- **Arrow Keys / WASD** — Steer your ship
- **Spacebar** — Fire
- Destroy all the asteroids without getting hit
- Repeat until the fun is cured (it never is)
